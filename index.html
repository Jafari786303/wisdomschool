About Wisdom School

A school platform designed for academic clarity, discipline, and communication.

Wisdom School is built for families who want a professional, structured, and supportive learning environment. Our platform is not only a school website. It is a digital school front desk, admissions office, classroom assistant, and messaging center combined into one experience.

We provide a strong foundation in Mathematics, Science, General Science, Tajweed, Geography, Biology, Computer Studies, Islamiat, English, and other guided subjects. Students are placed by grade and course interest, and teachers are matched based on the exact grades and subjects they can teach.

Programs And Learning Paths

Multi-subject study options with room for school-wide and class-specific learning.

Students can build a balanced plan from academic, technical, and religious learning areas while teachers manage grade fit and subject expertise from the same platform.

Core Academics

Mathematics, Science, General Science, Geography, Biology, English, and Islamiat.

Technology

Computer fundamentals, digital literacy, structured assignments, and progress review.

Faith And Language

Tajweed, Quran reading support, character development, and communication practice.

Teacher Support

Teacher application intake, grade matching, student selection, and group messaging.

School Community

Built-in communication spaces for class discussion, teacher guidance, and leadership contact.

Every approved account can join the principal chat, a whole-school general group, and a class group. Teachers can search students by profile name or email and open direct chats.

Default School Rooms

  • Principal Chat for school-wide announcements and direct support
  • Whole School General Group for community discussion and updates
  • Whole Class General Group for teacher-to-class communication

Student Dashboard Features

  • Modern messenger layout with searchable chat list
  • Task board with status labels
  • Lesson overview cards
  • Score table for recent performance

Teacher Workspace Features

  • Student profile list with grade and course details
  • Ability to select students for teaching
  • Open direct chats from search or profile selection
  • Operate with minimal data writes for the Firebase free plan
